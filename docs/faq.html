Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about PocketDemon

General

What is PocketDemon?

PocketDemon is a comprehensive concert tracking app that helps you log your concert history, discover statistics about your musical journey, track bands you've seen, and connect with friends who share your passion for live music.

Is PocketDemon free?

PocketDemon is free to use with all core features available. Some advanced features may be available through optional in-app purchases or a premium subscription in the future.

What platforms is PocketDemon available on?

PocketDemon is available on:

  • Android (phones and tablets)
  • iOS (iPhone and iPad)
  • Windows (desktop)
  • macOS (desktop)
  • Linux (desktop)
  • Web (browser-based)

Does my data sync across devices?

Yes! When you enable cloud backup (via Dropbox), your data syncs across all your devices. You can also manually export and import data between devices.

Getting Started

How do I add my first concert?

From the home screen, tap the + button and select "Add Concert." Enter the event name, date, venue, and bands you saw. You can add as much or as little detail as you like.

Can I import my concert history from another app?

Yes! PocketDemon supports importing from:

  • Setlist.fm (your attended shows)
  • Last.fm (your event history)
  • CSV files (from spreadsheets)
  • Other concert tracking apps (via CSV export)

See the Import Data section for detailed instructions.

How do I add past concerts?

You can add concerts from any date in the past. When creating a concert entry, simply select the historical date. PocketDemon doesn't restrict you to only logging current or future events.

Data & Privacy

Where is my data stored?

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud backup, encrypted copies are stored in your connected cloud service (Dropbox). We do not store your personal concert data on our servers.

Can I export all my data?

Yes! Go to Settings > Data Management > Export to download a complete backup of your data in JSON or CSV format. You own your data and can take it with you anytime.

What happens if I lose my phone?

If you have cloud backup enabled, you can restore all your data on a new device. Simply install PocketDemon, connect to your Dropbox account, and restore from backup. This is why we strongly recommend enabling cloud backup.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Data Management > Delete > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from the app and any connected cloud services. This action cannot be undone.

Features

What's the difference between an Event and a Concert?

An Event is the show itself (e.g., "Summer Fest 2024" or "Band X Tour"). A Concert is your attendance at that event. Events can have multiple concerts if you attend multiple days or the same band at different venues.

How do band statistics work?

PocketDemon tracks every band you've seen. When you add bands to your concerts, they're added to your "seen" list with statistics like how many times you've seen them, first/last time seen, and which venues/events.

Can I track bands I want to see?

Yes! Use the Wishlist feature to track bands you want to see. You can get notifications when they announce tours in your area.

How does band enrichment work?

When you add a band, PocketDemon can automatically fetch additional information like genres, country of origin, formed year, and photos from music databases like MusicBrainz. This helps complete your band profiles without manual entry.

What are achievements?

Achievements are badges you earn for various milestones in your concert journey, such as seeing your first concert, visiting 10 different venues, or seeing 50 unique bands. Check the Achievements page for the complete list.

Social Features

How do I connect with friends?

Use the Buddies feature to connect with friends. You can search by username, scan QR codes in person, or share invite links.

What can buddies see about my activity?

You control what buddies can see through sharing levels:

  • Minimal - Just that you're connected
  • Basic - Concert count and favorites
  • Standard - Concert list and basic stats
  • Full - Complete history and details

Can I share my concert stats on social media?

Yes! PocketDemon generates beautiful share cards that you can post to any social media platform. See the Sharing section for details.

Technical Issues

The app is running slowly. What can I do?

Try these steps:

  1. Clear the app cache: Settings > Data Management > Storage > Clear Cache
  2. Optimize storage: Settings > Data Management > Storage > Optimize
  3. Restart the app
  4. If the issue persists, try reinstalling (after backing up your data)

Why isn't band enrichment finding my band?

Band enrichment relies on external databases. If a band isn't found:

  • Check the spelling of the band name
  • Try alternative spellings or the full band name
  • Very new or local bands may not be in the database yet
  • You can always add band details manually

Maps aren't loading. What's wrong?

Maps require an internet connection. If you're offline, only downloaded offline maps will be available. Check your internet connection and try again. You can also try Settings > Maps > Clear Map Cache.

My backup isn't syncing. How do I fix it?

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Verify your Dropbox account is still connected
  3. Try manually triggering a backup
  4. Check if Dropbox has sufficient storage space
  5. Disconnect and reconnect the Dropbox integration

Tips & Tricks

What's the fastest way to log a concert?

Use the Quick Add feature from the home screen. You can also import concerts directly from setlist.fm if they have the show listed.

Can I log concerts while offline?

Yes! PocketDemon works fully offline. All data is stored locally and will sync when you're back online (if cloud backup is enabled).

How do I find concerts I might have forgotten?

Use the import features to check your Last.fm or setlist.fm history. You might discover concerts you forgot to log!

Contact & Support

How do I report a bug?

Go to Settings > Help & Support > Report a Bug. Include as much detail as possible about what happened, what you expected, and steps to reproduce the issue.

How do I request a new feature?

Go to Settings > Help & Support > Feature Request. We love hearing ideas from our users!

Where can I get more help?

Beyond this manual, you can:

  • Contact support through the app
  • Check the community forums
  • Follow us on social media for updates
