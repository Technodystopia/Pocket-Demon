Merch Tracker

Keep track of all your concert merchandise purchases

Overview

The Merch Tracker helps you catalog and organize all the merchandise you've bought at concerts, from t-shirts to posters to limited edition items.

Merch collection overview

Adding Merch Items

Quick Add from Concert

  1. Open a concert entry
  2. Scroll to Merch section
  3. Tap Add Merch
  4. Select item type and enter details

From Merch Screen

  1. Go to Merch from main menu
  2. Tap the + button
  3. Link to a concert (optional)
  4. Enter item details

Item Types

Categorize your merch by type:

Apparel

  • T-shirts
  • Hoodies
  • Jackets
  • Hats
  • Patches
  • Pins/Buttons

Media

  • Vinyl records
  • CDs
  • Cassettes
  • DVDs/Blu-rays

Art & Prints

  • Posters
  • Screen prints
  • Setlists
  • Signed items

Other

  • Stickers
  • Guitar picks
  • Drumsticks
  • Other memorabilia

Item Details

Track comprehensive details for each item:

Basic Info

  • Name/Description - What is it?
  • Band - Which band's merch
  • Type - Category of item
  • Size - For apparel items
  • Color - Item color

Purchase Details

  • Price - What you paid
  • Date - When you bought it
  • Concert - Link to specific show
  • Location - Venue or online store

Special Attributes

  • Limited edition - Flag rare items
  • Numbered - For numbered prints (#/500)
  • Signed - Autographed items
  • Tour exclusive - Only sold on tour
  • Condition - Rate item condition

Photos

Add photos of your merch items. Great for:

  • Insurance documentation
  • Showing off unique items
  • Tracking wear/condition over time

Organizing Merch

Views

  • Gallery - Visual grid of items with photos
  • List - Compact text listing
  • By Band - Grouped by artist
  • By Concert - Grouped by show

Filters

  • Item type
  • Band
  • Year purchased
  • Price range
  • Size (for apparel)
  • Special items only

Sort Options

  • Date added
  • Purchase date
  • Band name
  • Price
  • Item type

Merch Statistics

See insights about your merch spending:

  • Total spent - All-time merch spending
  • By year - Annual spending breakdown
  • By band - Which bands get your money
  • By type - T-shirts vs vinyl vs other
  • Average per show - How much you typically spend
  • Most expensive item - Your priciest purchase

Merch spending statistics

Apparel Inventory

Special features for clothing items:

Size Tracking

  • Set your default size
  • Track items in different sizes
  • Filter by size to see what fits

Wear Tracking

  • Mark items as "worn" or "unworn"
  • Track last worn date
  • See your most/least worn items

Closet Mode

Can't decide what to wear? Use the "Random Pick" feature to get a random shirt suggestion from your collection!

Limited Edition Tracking

Special handling for rare items:

  • Flag items as limited edition
  • Track edition numbers (/250, /500, etc.)
  • Document authenticity (certificates, receipts)
  • Link to photos of packaging/tags

Integration with Spending

Merch purchases automatically feed into your spending tracker:

  • Merch costs added to concert total spending
  • Category breakdown in spending reports
  • Year-over-year comparison

Export Options

  • CSV - Full inventory spreadsheet
  • Insurance report - Documented list with values and photos
  • Collection summary - Overview for sharing
