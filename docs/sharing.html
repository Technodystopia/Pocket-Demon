Sharing Your Concert Life

Share your concert experiences with friends and on social media

Share Cards

PocketDemon generates beautiful share cards that you can post to social media or send to friends.

Concert Share Cards

After attending a concert, create a shareable card that includes:

  • Event name and date
  • Venue information
  • Your rating and review
  • Band lineup
  • Custom background options

Concert share card preview

Statistics Cards

Share your concert statistics:

  • Overall stats - Total concerts, bands, venues
  • Genre breakdown - Your musical journey visualized
  • Milestone celebrations - Share when you hit achievement milestones

How to Share

  1. Navigate to the item you want to share (concert, stats, achievement)
  2. Tap the Share icon (usually in the top right)
  3. Choose your card style and customize options
  4. Select your sharing destination:
    • Save to device
    • Share to social media
    • Copy to clipboard
    • Send via messaging apps

Share Card Customization

Backgrounds

Choose from several background options:

  • Dark gradient - Sleek, modern look
  • Band colors - Based on band artwork
  • Venue photo - If available from venue data
  • Plain colors - Simple, clean options

Information Display

Toggle what information appears on your share card:

  • Show/hide ratings
  • Show/hide venue details
  • Include/exclude your username
  • Add concert number (e.g., "Concert #150")

Buddy Sharing

Share directly with your PocketDemon buddies:

Concert Memories

  • Share concert entries with buddies who attended the same show
  • Compare ratings and reviews
  • See each other's photos (if shared)

Band Recommendations

  • Send band recommendations to buddies
  • Include your notes about why they might like the band
  • Link directly to the band's page in their app

Sharing Permissions

What buddies see depends on your sharing level settings. Review your buddy privacy settings in the Buddies section.

Social Media Tips

Best Practices

  • Instagram Stories - Share cards are sized perfectly for stories
  • Twitter/X - Cards include metadata for nice link previews
  • Facebook - Tag venues and bands for more engagement

Hashtags

PocketDemon can automatically suggest relevant hashtags:

  • Band name hashtags
  • Venue hashtags
  • Genre hashtags
  • Event-specific tags (festival names, tour names)

QR Code Sharing

Generate QR codes to share your concert profile:

  • Profile QR - Others can scan to add you as a buddy
  • Concert QR - Share a specific concert entry
  • Stats QR - Link to your public statistics page

At Shows

Use the QR code feature at concerts to quickly connect with other PocketDemon users you meet!
