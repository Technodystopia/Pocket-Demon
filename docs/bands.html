Bands

Managing Your Band Collection

The Bands tab shows all bands in your collection - every band you've seen live or added to your wishlist.

Adding a New Band

  1. Go to the Bands tab
  2. Tap the + button
  3. Enter the band name
  4. Optionally set country of origin and genres
  5. Tap Save

Tip

Bands are usually created automatically when you add them to an event. You rarely need to create bands manually.

Band Details

Tap any band to see their detail page with:

  • Photo & Logo - Artist image and band logo (from TheAudioDB)
  • Basic Info - Country, formation year, genres, status
  • Concert History - All times you've seen this band
  • Statistics - Times seen, average rating, first/last seen
  • External Links - Wikipedia, Discogs, Spotify, etc.
  • Notes - Your personal notes about this band
  • Gallery - Photos from events featuring this band

Enriching Band Data

PocketDemon can fetch additional information from online databases:

MusicBrainz

Provides structured metadata:

  • Official formation and disbandment years
  • Country and area of origin
  • Official genres and aliases
  • External links (Wikipedia, Discogs, AllMusic, Last.fm)
  • Social links (Spotify, YouTube, Facebook, etc.)

TheAudioDB

Provides visual assets:

  • Artist photos
  • Band logos
  • Biography text
  • Style/sub-genre information

To enrich a band:

  1. Open the band's detail page
  2. Tap the Enrich button (or menu → Enrich)
  3. Wait for the data to be fetched
  4. Review and confirm the updates

Band Status Icons

Bands can have special status indicators:

Icon Status Description
❤ Favorite Your favorite bands - appears in Ticketmaster scanner
★ Wishlist Bands you want to see live
🚫 Ignored Hidden from suggestions and imports
⚑ Flagged Marked for review or attention

Band Notes

Add personal notes to any band:

  1. Open the band's detail page
  2. Scroll to the Notes section
  3. Tap to edit
  4. Enter your notes (favorite songs, memories, etc.)
  5. Tap Save

Searching & Filtering

Use the search bar to find bands by:

  • Band name (partial matches work)
  • Country of origin
  • Genre

Filter options include:

  • All Bands - Everything in your collection
  • Favorites - Only favorite bands
  • Wishlist - Bands you want to see
  • Most Seen - Sorted by concert count

Band Actions

From the band detail page, you can:

  • Add to Event - Log a new concert for this band
  • Add to Wishlist - Mark as want-to-see
  • Toggle Favorite - Mark as a favorite
  • Edit - Update band information
  • Share - Share with buddies
  • Delete - Remove from collection (if no concerts)

Warning

You cannot delete a band that has concert records. Delete the concerts first, or the band will remain in your collection.

Genre Management

Genres are stored as a comma-separated list. Common examples:

  • Heavy Metal, Thrash Metal
  • Rock, Alternative Rock
  • Electronic, Industrial

Genres are used for:

  • Statistics and charts (genre diversity, evolution)
  • Band Origins map filtering
  • Year in Review breakdowns
  • Spending by genre analysis
