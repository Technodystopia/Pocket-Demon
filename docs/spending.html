Spending Tracker

Track all your concert-related expenses in one place

Overview

The Spending Tracker gives you complete visibility into your concert-going expenses, from tickets to travel to merch.

Spending overview dashboard

Expense Categories

Track expenses across multiple categories:

Tickets

  • Ticket price
  • Service fees
  • VIP upgrades
  • Meet & greet packages

Travel

  • Gas/fuel
  • Flights
  • Parking
  • Rideshare/taxi
  • Public transit

Accommodation

  • Hotels
  • Airbnb
  • Camping fees

At the Show

  • Merchandise
  • Food & drinks
  • Coat check
  • Tips

Adding Expenses

From a Concert

  1. Open the concert entry
  2. Scroll to Spending section
  3. Tap Add Expense
  4. Select category and enter amount

Quick Add

  1. Go to Spending from main menu
  2. Tap the + button
  3. Select or search for the concert
  4. Add expense details

Expense Details

  • Amount - How much you spent
  • Category - Type of expense
  • Date - When you spent it
  • Notes - Additional details
  • Receipt photo - Attach a photo

Currency Support

For international concert-goers:

  • Enter expenses in any currency
  • Automatic conversion to your home currency
  • Historical exchange rates for accuracy
  • View totals in any currency

Setting Your Currency

Set your default currency in Settings > Preferences > Currency. All reports will show totals in this currency.

Spending Reports

Dashboard Overview

  • Total spent - All-time spending
  • This year - Current year total
  • Average per concert - Your typical spend
  • Highest single concert - Your biggest expense

Category Breakdown

See how your money is distributed:

  • Pie chart by category
  • Tickets vs merch vs travel
  • Trends over time

Spending category breakdown

Time-Based Reports

  • Monthly - Spending by month
  • Yearly - Year-over-year comparison
  • By festival - Multi-day event breakdown

By Band/Venue

  • Which bands cost you most to see
  • Most expensive venues
  • Cost comparison across tours

Budget Features

Set Budgets

  • Annual budget - Set a yearly spending goal
  • Per-concert budget - Typical concert limit
  • Category budgets - Limit merch spending, etc.

Budget Tracking

  • See budget remaining
  • Get alerts when approaching limits
  • Track progress throughout the year

Smart Budgeting

PocketDemon can suggest budgets based on your historical spending patterns. Check Settings > Spending > Suggest Budget.

Cost Per Show Analysis

Understand your true cost per concert:

Metrics

  • Cost per band - Total spent / bands seen
  • Cost per hour - Including travel and wait time
  • Festival value - Compare multi-day vs single shows

Efficiency Score

PocketDemon calculates a "value score" based on:

  • Number of bands seen
  • Total time at venue
  • Total cost
  • Your ratings

Receipt Management

  • Attach receipt photos to expenses
  • Organize receipts by concert
  • Export receipts for reimbursement
  • Searchable receipt archive

Export Options

  • CSV - Full expense spreadsheet
  • PDF report - Formatted spending report
  • By date range - Custom period exports
  • Tax-ready - If concerts are business-related

Shared Expenses

For concerts with friends:

  • Mark expenses as shared
  • Track who paid vs who owes
  • Split calculations
  • Settlement tracking
