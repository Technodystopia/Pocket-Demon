Photo Gallery

Store and organize your concert photos and memories

Overview

The Gallery feature lets you attach photos to your concert entries, creating a visual record of your concert-going journey.

Gallery grid view

Adding Photos

From a Concert Entry

  1. Open any concert in your history
  2. Scroll to the Photos section
  3. Tap Add Photos
  4. Choose from:
    • Camera - Take a new photo
    • Gallery - Select from your device
    • Files - Import from file storage

Batch Import

Add multiple photos at once:

  1. Go to Gallery from the main menu
  2. Tap the + button
  3. Select multiple photos
  4. PocketDemon will attempt to match photos to concerts based on:
    • Photo date/time metadata
    • Location data (if available)
  5. Review and confirm matches

Gallery Views

Grid View

See all your photos in a scrollable grid. Tap any photo to view it full-screen.

Timeline View

Photos organized chronologically with concert dates as headers.

By Concert

Browse photos grouped by the concert they're attached to.

By Venue

See all photos taken at a specific venue across all your visits.

By Band

View all photos from concerts featuring a specific band.

Photo Details

Each photo can include:

  • Caption - Add a description or memory
  • Tags - Tag bands visible in the photo
  • Favorite - Mark your best shots
  • Location - Automatically captured or manually set

Pro Tip

Tag bands in your photos to easily find them later. "Show me all photos of [Band Name]" becomes possible!

Storage Options

Local Storage

Photos are stored on your device by default. This provides:

  • Full resolution images
  • Offline access
  • No cloud dependency

Cloud Backup

If you have Dropbox integration enabled:

  • Photos are backed up automatically
  • Sync across devices
  • Recoverable if you lose your device

Storage Space

Photos can take up significant storage space. Consider enabling cloud backup and using the "Optimize Storage" option in Settings to keep thumbnails locally while storing full-resolution images in the cloud.

Memories Feature

PocketDemon creates automatic "Memories" from your gallery:

On This Day

See photos from concerts on this date in previous years.

Year Collections

Automatic albums for each year of concert-going.

Best Of

Your favorited photos compiled into a highlight reel.

Festival Memories

Photos from multi-day events grouped together.

Sharing Photos

  • Individual photos - Share any photo directly
  • Photo sets - Share multiple photos as a collection
  • With buddies - Share photos with connected buddies
  • To social media - Export with PocketDemon branding

Managing Storage

Optimize Storage

Settings > Storage > Optimize Photos

  • Keeps thumbnails on device
  • Full resolution stored in cloud
  • Downloads full image when needed

Delete Options

  • Delete from concert - Removes photo from concert but keeps file
  • Delete permanently - Removes photo completely
  • Bulk delete - Select multiple photos to remove
