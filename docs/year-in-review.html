Year in Review

Celebrate your concert year with a personalized recap

Overview

At the end of each year (or anytime you want), generate a comprehensive Year in Review that celebrates your concert-going journey.

Year in Review opening screen

Accessing Year in Review

  1. Go to Statistics from the main menu
  2. Tap Year in Review
  3. Select the year to review
  4. Swipe through your personalized story

Availability

Year in Review is available for any year where you have at least 1 concert logged. More concerts = more detailed insights!

What's Included

The Numbers

  • Total concerts - How many shows you attended
  • Unique bands - Number of different artists seen
  • Venues visited - How many different venues
  • Cities/Countries - Geographic spread
  • Total hours - Estimated time at shows

Highlights

  • First concert of the year - Your year opener
  • Last concert of the year - Your year closer
  • Highest rated show - Your top concert
  • Most seen band - Who you saw the most
  • Most visited venue - Your home base

Genre Journey

  • Breakdown of genres seen
  • How your tastes evolved through the year
  • New genres explored

Travel Stats

  • Miles/kilometers traveled for concerts
  • Furthest concert from home
  • Map of all venues visited
  • New cities discovered

Personal Insights

Concert Persona

Based on your activity, PocketDemon assigns you a "persona":

  • Festival Warrior - Lots of festival attendance
  • Local Legend - Supporting the local scene
  • Road Warrior - Traveling far for shows
  • Genre Explorer - Diverse musical tastes
  • Loyalty Lifer - Seeing favorites multiple times
  • Front Row Fanatic - High attendance frequency

Milestones Reached

  • Achievements unlocked this year
  • Concert count milestones (50th, 100th, etc.)
  • Personal records broken

Busiest Period

  • Your busiest month
  • Longest concert streak
  • Most concerts in a week

Spending Summary

Financial Overview

  • Total spent on concerts
  • Breakdown by category
  • Average per show
  • Comparison to previous year

Best Value

  • Best value concert (cost per band/hour)
  • Cheapest show attended
  • Most expensive outing

Memories

Photo Highlights

A curated selection of your best photos from the year.

Review Excerpts

Highlights from your concert reviews and notes.

On This Day

Flashbacks to concerts from previous years on the same dates.

Comparisons

Year Over Year

  • More or fewer concerts than last year?
  • New bands vs returning favorites
  • Genre shifts
  • Spending changes

All-Time Context

  • Where this year ranks in your history
  • Percentage of total concerts
  • Contribution to lifetime stats

Sharing Your Year

Share Cards

Generate shareable graphics:

  • Summary card with key stats
  • Top concerts list
  • Genre breakdown visualization
  • Map of venues visited

Full Report

Export your complete Year in Review:

  • PDF - Printable report
  • Image series - Social media friendly
  • Web link - Shareable online version

Year in Review share card

Interactive Features

Story Mode

Swipe through your year like a story:

  • Animated transitions
  • Music snippets (if Spotify connected)
  • Photo memories
  • Achievement celebrations

Deep Dive

Tap any stat to see more detail:

  • Full list of concerts
  • Complete band list
  • All venues visited
  • Detailed spending breakdown

Previous Years

Access Year in Review for any past year:

  1. Go to Year in Review
  2. Tap the year selector at the top
  3. Choose any year with concert data

Decade in Review

After 10 years of use, unlock the special "Decade in Review" feature for an even bigger celebration of your concert journey!
