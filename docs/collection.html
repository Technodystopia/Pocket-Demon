Physical Collection

Catalog your vinyl, CDs, tapes, and other physical music media

Overview

The Collection feature helps you catalog and organize your physical music collection. Track your vinyl records, CDs, cassettes, and other formats all in one place.

Collection overview

Adding Items

Manual Entry

  1. Go to Collection from the main menu
  2. Tap the + button
  3. Enter the details:
    • Artist/Band
    • Album title
    • Format (vinyl, CD, cassette, etc.)
    • Year of release
    • Condition
    • Purchase details (optional)

Barcode Scanning

  1. Tap the Scan button
  2. Point your camera at the barcode
  3. PocketDemon looks up the item in the Discogs database
  4. Review and confirm the match
  5. Add any additional details

Discogs Import

Import your entire Discogs collection:

  1. Go to Settings > Integrations > Discogs
  2. Connect your Discogs account
  3. Tap Import Collection
  4. Select which items to import

Discogs Sync

Once connected, your Discogs collection stays in sync. Add items in either app and they'll appear in both.

Item Details

Each collection item can include:

Basic Info

  • Artist - Linked to your bands database
  • Title - Album or release name
  • Format - Vinyl, CD, Cassette, 8-track, etc.
  • Variant - Color vinyl, limited edition, etc.
  • Year - Original release or pressing year

Condition

Rate condition using Discogs/Goldmine standards:

  • M - Mint (perfect, unplayed)
  • NM - Near Mint (nearly perfect)
  • VG+ - Very Good Plus (minor wear)
  • VG - Very Good (noticeable wear)
  • G+ - Good Plus (significant wear)
  • G - Good (plays through)
  • F - Fair (major issues)
  • P - Poor (barely plays)

Purchase Info

  • Price paid - What you spent
  • Purchase date - When you bought it
  • Purchase location - Where you bought it
  • Current value - Auto-fetched from Discogs

Storage

  • Location - Where it's stored (shelf, crate, etc.)
  • Notes - Any additional information

Organizing Your Collection

Views

  • Grid - Album artwork display
  • List - Compact listing
  • Spine - Vinyl spine view (like a shelf)

Sort Options

  • Artist name (A-Z)
  • Album title
  • Date added
  • Year of release
  • Format
  • Value

Filters

  • By format
  • By genre
  • By decade
  • By condition
  • By storage location

Collection Statistics

Track your collection growth:

  • Total items - Overall collection size
  • By format - Breakdown of formats
  • Total value - Estimated collection worth
  • Growth over time - How your collection has grown
  • Top artists - Who you have the most releases from
  • Genre distribution - What genres dominate your collection

Collection statistics

Concert Connection

PocketDemon links your collection to your concert history:

  • See which albums you own from bands you've seen live
  • Track vinyl purchased at shows
  • View collection items on band pages
  • Get suggestions for bands you've seen but don't own

At the Merch Table

Check if you already own an album before buying at a show. Open the band's page to see your collection items.

Wishlist Integration

Your collection wishlist works alongside your concert wishlist:

  • Add albums you want to your collection wishlist
  • Get alerts when wishlist items are available at Discogs
  • Track price changes on wanted items
  • Move items from wishlist to collection when purchased

Export Options

  • CSV export - Full collection spreadsheet
  • Discogs sync - Keep Discogs updated
  • Insurance report - Detailed list with values for insurance
  • Trade list - Generate a list of items for trade
