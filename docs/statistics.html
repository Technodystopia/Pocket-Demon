Statistics

Your Concert Data, Visualized

The Statistics tab provides insights into your concert history with charts, numbers, and trends.

Note

Statistics only include past events. Future events are excluded from all calculations.

Overview Tab

Quick glance at your concert journey:

  • Total Concerts - How many bands you've seen
  • Total Events - Shows you've attended
  • Unique Bands - Different bands in your collection
  • Countries - Countries where you've seen concerts
  • On This Day - Concerts on today's date in past years
  • Concert Streaks - Consecutive days/weeks/months with shows

Bands Tab

Band-focused statistics:

Most Seen Bands

Ranked list of bands by concert count. Tap to see when and where.

Genre Diversity

Pie chart showing your genre distribution. How diverse is your taste?

Genre Evolution

Stacked area chart showing how your genre preferences have changed over the years.

Band History

  • Oldest Bands - Bands by formation year
  • Youngest Bands - Newest bands you've seen
  • By Decade - 70s, 80s, 90s, 2000s, 2010s, 2020s bands
  • Active vs Disbanded - Current status breakdown

Events Tab

Event-level statistics:

  • All Events - Complete event list
  • Festivals - Multi-day festival events
  • Single Shows - One-night concerts
  • Multi-Day - Events spanning multiple days
  • Cruises - Cruise ship concerts
  • International - Events outside your home country

Venues Tab

Venue statistics:

  • Top Venues - Ranked by visit count
  • Unique Venues - Total different venues visited
  • Cities - Cities where you've seen concerts
  • Venue Types - Clubs, arenas, outdoor, festivals

Trends Tab

See patterns in your concert attendance:

Year Comparison

Bar chart comparing concerts per year. Your most active years at a glance.

Monthly Heatmap

Grid showing concert density by month across years. Find your peak concert season.

Day of Week

Which days do you go to concerts most? Saturday winner?

Calendar View

Visual calendar marking all concert dates.

Distance Traveled

Track how far you've traveled for music:

  • Lifetime Distance - Total kilometers/miles traveled
  • Yearly Breakdown - Distance per year
  • Furthest Concert - Your longest trip
  • Average Distance - Per concert average

Tip

Set your home location in Settings to calculate distances accurately.

Setlist Statistics

If you've viewed setlists, see song statistics:

  • Most Heard Songs - Songs you've heard live most often
  • Rare Songs - Uncommon songs you've witnessed
  • Covers - Cover songs performed
  • Total Songs - All songs across all setlists

Understanding the Numbers

What Counts as a "Concert"?

Each band-at-event combination. If you saw 5 bands at a festival, that's 5 concerts.

What Counts as an "Event"?

A single show entry - could be one night or a multi-day festival.

How is Distance Calculated?

Straight-line distance from your home location to the venue's GPS coordinates.

How are Genres Counted?

Each band's genres are counted once per band (not per concert). A band with "Heavy Metal, Thrash Metal" adds to both categories.

Performance Notes

Statistics are computed on-demand using isolate processing. For large collections (1000+ concerts), there may be a brief loading time when switching tabs.

Heavy computations are cached during the session for fast repeat access.
