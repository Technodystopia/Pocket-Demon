Maps

Visualize Your Concert Journey

PocketDemon includes several maps to explore your concert history geographically.

Concert Map

See all your concerts plotted on a world map.

  • Markers show venue locations
  • Clustering groups nearby venues
  • Tap a marker for venue details
  • Color coding by visit count

How to Use

  1. Go to More → Maps
  2. Select Concert Map
  3. Pinch to zoom, drag to pan
  4. Tap clusters to expand
  5. Tap individual markers for details

Band Origins Map

Explore where your bands come from with four views:

Heatmap Tab

Choropleth map showing band density by country. Darker colors = more bands.

Chart Tab

Bar chart of all countries ranked by band count. See your full geographic diversity.

Missing Tab

Countries you haven't seen bands from yet:

  • Shows country flags
  • Tap any country for band suggestions
  • Suggestions come from 329K+ band database
  • Quick-add bands to your wishlist

World Map Tab

Pins showing band locations by their area/city of origin (when available from MusicBrainz).

Venue Map

All venues you've visited on one map:

  • Grouped by city
  • Shows visit count per venue
  • Tap for venue details
  • Get directions to any venue

Offline Map Caching

Download map tiles for offline use:

  1. Go to Settings → Maps
  2. Tap Download Tiles
  3. Select region and zoom levels
  4. Wait for download to complete

Tip

Download map tiles before festivals with poor connectivity. You'll be able to view maps without internet.

Cache Management

  • View cache size in Settings
  • Clear cache to free storage
  • Set maximum cache size
  • Auto-eviction of old tiles

Geographic Achievements

Unlock badges for your geographic reach:

Countries

  • Border Hopper - 3 countries
  • International Fan - 5 countries
  • Globe Trotter - 10 countries
  • World Traveler - 25 countries
  • Concert Nomad - 50 countries

Cities

  • City Explorer - 10 cities
  • Urban Adventurer - 25 cities
  • City Hopper - 50 cities
  • Metropolitan Master - 100 cities

Map Data Sources

  • Tiles - OpenStreetMap
  • Venue Coordinates - Ticketmaster, manual entry
  • Band Origins - MusicBrainz area data
  • Country Boundaries - Natural Earth Data
