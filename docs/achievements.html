Achievements

Earn Badges for Your Concert Journey

PocketDemon includes 32 badges across 7 categories. Badges are automatically awarded when you reach milestones.

Viewing Achievements

  1. Go to More → Achievements
  2. Or use the quick action from the Home screen
  3. Browse by category (collapsible sections)
  4. See progress toward locked badges

How Badges Work

  • Badges are checked automatically when data changes
  • Once earned, badges cannot be lost
  • Progress is shown for locked badges
  • Unlock dates are recorded

Tip

Check the Achievements screen after importing data - you may unlock several badges at once!
