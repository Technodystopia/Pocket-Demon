Key Highlights

🔒 Privacy First

All data stored locally on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads. Optional Dropbox sync if you want cloud backup.

🌐 Works Offline

Full functionality without internet. Perfect for festivals with poor connectivity. Offline map caching available.

💰 No Subscription

Buy once, use forever. No monthly fees, no premium tiers, no locked features.

📦 Your Data

Export everything as JSON or CSV. Import from setlist.fm, Last.fm, and Discogs. You own your data.
